# Pricing Policy

## Subscription Tiers
We offer four main subscription tiers:
- **Free**: Basic features, community support.
- **Standard**: Core features, email support, $49/month per seat.
- **Pro**: Advanced features, priority support, $99/month per seat.
- **Enterprise**: Custom features, dedicated account manager, custom pricing.

## Discounts
- **Non-Profit Discount**: We offer a 30% discount off the Standard tier for verified non-profit organizations.

## Billing Rules
- **Pro-Rata Billing**: If you upgrade your plan mid-billing cycle, you will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current cycle. If you downgrade, the new rate takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle. No prorated refunds are issued for downgrades.

## Enterprise Custom Pricing
Enterprise pricing is custom and requires a scoping call. Custom pricing includes dedicated infrastructure and custom SLA agreements. Contact sales for a quote.

# Refund and Retention Policy

## Refund Rules
- **14-Day Window**: No refunds are provided after 14 days from the date of initial purchase or renewal.
- **Exception Process**: Exceptions can only be approved by the billing manager. A customer must submit a detailed explanation of the extenuating circumstances.
- **Credits vs Refunds**: Whenever possible, support agents should offer service credits instead of cash refunds to resolve billing disputes.

## Churn Retention Playbook
When a customer threatens to cancel or churn (e.g., negative sentiment, threatening public reviews), follow this retention playbook:
1. **Acknowledge and Empathize**: Validate their frustration immediately.
2. **First Offer**: Offer a 20% discount on their next 3 months of service if they stay.
3. **Escalation Offer (VIP/High Risk)**: If they reject the first offer and are a high-value account, offer 1 free month of service + a 1-on-1 onboarding call.
4. If they still demand to leave, process the cancellation gracefully and ask for exit feedback.

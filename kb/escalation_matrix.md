# Escalation Matrix

When an agent or support representative encounters high-risk scenarios, they must escalate to the appropriate internal team based on the matrix below:

## Legal Threats
Any communication threatening a lawsuit, referencing "legal counsel", "cease and desist", or "breach of contract" must be escalated to the **Legal Team**.

## Security Incidents
Reports of data breaches, suspicious logins, ransomware demands (e.g., "send Bitcoin"), or vulnerabilities must be routed to the **Security Team** immediately. Never auto-reply to a ransom demand.

## PR Crises
Threats to post negative reviews publicly (e.g., Trustpilot, G2, Twitter/X) or journalists asking for comment must be escalated to the **PR / Communications Team**.

## VIP Churns
If an Enterprise or VIP account threatens to cancel their subscription, immediately escalate to the **Customer Success Manager (CSM)** or Account Manager.

## GDPR Requests
Formal GDPR requests, including "Right to be Forgotten" or "Article 20 Data Portability" requests, must be escalated to the **Compliance Team** to ensure we hit our 30-day statutory response window.

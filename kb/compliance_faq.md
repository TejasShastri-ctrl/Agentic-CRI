# Compliance FAQ

## HIPAA BAA Availability
We offer a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for customers in the healthcare industry. This is only available to customers on the **Enterprise tier**.

## GDPR DPA Process
We are fully GDPR compliant. Customers can sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) within the dashboard under Settings > Legal.
**Article 20 Data Portability:** Upon receiving a formal data portability request, we are legally obligated to provide a full export of the user's data in a machine-readable format within the **30-day statutory window**.

## SOC 2 Type II Status
We have successfully completed our SOC 2 Type II audit. The compliance report is available to Pro and Enterprise customers under strict NDA.

## Data Residency Options
By default, all data is hosted in US-East. Enterprise customers can request localized data residency in the EU (Frankfurt) or APAC (Sydney) regions during onboarding.

# Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy

## Uptime SLA
We guarantee a **99.9% uptime** SLA for all Pro and Enterprise customers.

## Incident Response Times
- **P0 (Critical Outage)**: 15 minutes
- **P1 (High Impact)**: 1 hour
- **P2 (Medium/Low Impact)**: 12 hours

## Credit Calculation Formula
If we fail to meet our 99.9% uptime SLA in a given month, affected customers are eligible for service credits.
The credit calculation formula is:
- **99.0% - 99.89% uptime**: 10% credit of the monthly fee
- **95.0% - 98.99% uptime**: 25% credit of the monthly fee
- **Below 95.0% uptime**: 50% credit of the monthly fee

## RCA Delivery SLA
For any P0 Critical Outage, a formal Root Cause Analysis (RCA) report will be delivered to affected Enterprise customers within **24 hours** of the incident resolution.
